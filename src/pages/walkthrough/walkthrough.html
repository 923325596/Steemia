Welcome to Steemia

Steemia is a social networking app built on the decentralized Steem Blockchain, that reward users in Steem or Steem Dollars Cryptocurrencies.

Notifications

Get notifications regarding transfers and/or discussions (comments) you are involved in as well as notifications when someone follows you.

Bookmarks

Saw an article of your interest but want to save it for later? We got you! Save it in your bookmark and access it at any time.

Wallet

Keep tracking of all your Steem and Steem dollars and also feel free to watch your BTC, LTC, and ETH wallets as well!

Ready to get started?

Start getting rewards for your effort in good content. Post and vote articles of your interest and earn rewards from them.
